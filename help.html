Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long does shipping take?

Domestic orders: 3-5 business days. International orders: 7-14 business days.

2. Can I retursn or exchange an item?

Yes, unworn items can be returned within 30 days. For exchanges, contact customer support first.

3. Do you offer custom jewelry?

Yes, we design custom rings, necklaces, and bracelets. Send us your idea through our Help page.

4. Is your jewelry hypoallergenic?

Most of our pieces are hypoallergenic and made from nickel-free materials. Check each product description for details.

5. Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes, we offer premium gift wrapping for $5. You can select this option at checkout.

6. How can I track my order?

Once shipped, you'll receive an email with a tracking link so you can follow your order's progress.
